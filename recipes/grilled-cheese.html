Grilled Cheese

Description

Few things warm up the spirit like a fresh, gooey, grilled cheese.

The following recipe ensures that your sandwich will be the talk of the town.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Turn on the stove burner to medium with a skillet on it
  2. Allow the butter and cheese to warm to room temperature
  3. Spread an even coating of butter on one side of each bread slice
  4. Place one piece of bread, butter side down, in the preheated pan
  5. Add the slice of cheese to the bread
  6. Place the other piece of bread on the cheese, butter side up
  7. After two minutes, or when the cheese has melted enough to hold the bread together, flip the sandwich
  8. Cook for about one more minute, until bread is toasted but not burnt
