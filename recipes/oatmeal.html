Oatmeal

Description

Want to start your day right? Well if so, I have a secret for you...

Oatmeal contains all the whole grains and nutrients you will need to be a breakfast hero.

Ingredients

  • 1 Packet of instant oatmeal
  • 2/3 cups of water or milk

Steps

  1. Pour the oatmeal into a bowl
  2. Bring the water or milk to a boil
  3. Pour the boiling liquid into the bowl
  4. Wait about 1 - 1 1/2 minutes, until the oatmeal has softened
  5. Allow to cool and add your favorite fruits or nut toppings
